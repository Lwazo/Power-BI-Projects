This Power BI HR Analytics Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of workforce trends, employee demographics, and attrition patterns within an organization. The dashboard enables HR professionals and business leaders to monitor key workforce metrics, identify factors contributing to employee turnover, and make data-driven decisions to improve employee retention and organizational performance.

## 1. Key Features
### Employee overview with total employees, active employees, attrition count, attrition rate, average age, and average experience.
### Department-wise workforce analysis across Research & Development, Sales, and Human Resources.
### Attrition analysis by department, salary range, gender, age group, and job role.
### Employee satisfaction insights to identify areas impacting retention.
### Experience-based attrition trends for workforce planning and talent management.
### Interactive filters for age groups and departments to support deeper analysis.

## 2. Tools & Technologies
### Power BI
### Power Query for data transformation
### DAX for calculated measures and KPIs
### Interactive visualizations and dashboard design

## 3. Business Impact

This dashboard helps organizations uncover workforce trends, understand the drivers of employee attrition, optimize retention strategies, and support strategic HR decision-making through actionable insights and real-time analytics.
